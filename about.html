About

THE CREW

Spilled Milk began as an idea amongst close friends right after graduating from NYU. John Bagers, Susan Trout, amd Timothy Minnows all shared a love for ice cream and wanted to share that passion with the city of Austin. Using unique flavors, a relaxed enviroment, and local produce they have runned Spilled Milk Icecream for going on 3 years now.

THE SPACE

When the store was being designed the idea came to split away from the traditional ice cream parlor look and instead create a space people would want to relax in and hang out. A small two-story store front near downtown became the ideal location for the shop. Using a minimal design while creating an inviting atmosphere. After opening the store has become a local favorite amongst locals.

THE TASTE

Spilled Milk is commited to letting local flavors shine. Using only the best produce and products from local farmers and businesses, we create our signature monthly flavors to reflect the pick of the season. Our ice cream is created in the back at the start of each day to keep it fresh and delicious!
